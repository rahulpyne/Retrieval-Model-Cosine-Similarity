the united nations framework convention on climate change unfccc these talks concern the period after the first commitment period of the kyoto protocol which expired at the end of 2012 negotiations have been mandated by the adoption of the bali road map and decision 1 cp.13 the bali action plan unfccc negotiations are conducted within two subsidiary bodies the ad hoc working group on long-term nations climate change conference taking place in december 2009 in copenhagen cop-15 negotiations are supported by a number of external processes including the g8 process a number of regional meetings
